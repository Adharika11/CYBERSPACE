ABOUT

Beyond a shadow of doubt,the invention of the Internet is one of man's greatest and spectacular achievements. It has truly revolutionised the world in terms of how we engage in buisness,recreates,socialise,educate ourselves and communicate with each other. However there are always two sides of a coin.

Our community "CYBERSPACE" aims to create an awareness about the cybercrime activities that can have a devastating impact on the life of the user.It also aim to enhance the knowledge about "THE ETHICAL USAGE OF CYBERSPACE AND THE INTERNET" to reduce the cyber-criminal activities. As an individual we must know about the ethics of the virtual world we spend most of our time in and also about the cybercrime attacks so as to be aware about the malicious activities taking place on this WORLD WIDE WEB in order to have a safe surfing!!

We also aim in creating awareness about how the cybersecurity plays an important role in preventing cyber-breaches and thus safeguarding the user from the hackers or attackers.The knowledge of cybersecurity helps people in preventing themselves from becoming a victim of cybercrime.
