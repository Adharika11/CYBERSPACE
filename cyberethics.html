CYBERETHICS - ENVISIONING CHARACTER EDUCATION IN CYBERSPACE

The term "cyber ethics" refers to a set of moral rules or a code of behaviour applied to the online environment.Cyberethics is a branch of computer technology behavior that defines the best practices that must be adopted by a user when he uses the computer system. In simple terms, cyberethics refers to the basic ethics and etiquette that must be followed while using a computer system. As a broad range of people are connected to each other through cyberspace, Cyber ethics play a major role in maintaining the decorum of the virtual world. Ethics are the values defining interactions and activities within the cyberspace i.e., propagating good and polite behaviour online that is not harsh and rude.It emphasises the users to use the internet safely and responsibly and thus protects the moral, financial and social behaviour of an individual.



CYBERSPACE FOCUSES ON THE FOLLOWING:-

PRIVACY:

The content that is available on the internet should not hurt any moral, emotional or personal ethics of the individual. Private information like user’s contact details, address, bank details, credit/debit card details, etc must not be breached in any case.

ACCURACY:

The content available on the internet is accessed by many users. Cyber ethics assert the importance of posting content on the internet that is correct and reliable in all aspects. It ensures that the information shared online must not mislead the public in any sense.

IPR:

IPR stands for INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS It defines that the owner had the complete right to the content that is posted on the internet. The content posted by someone originally belongs to him/her and nobody can claim that to be theirs. Cyber ethics tells us that unauthorised distribution of someone’s else work should not be adopted.

SECURITY:

Security is the most important right that every individual should be assured with while surfing the internet. The confidential information must be highly protected without any risk of its loss. Cyber ethics ensures that each and every individual can access the internet safely.

CYBER ETHIC CONDUCT: -

BEST policies that an individual must adapt while using the internet or any kind of technology in order to maintain a good cyber ethic conduct and prevent cyber ethic breaches are: -

  • AVOID clicking unknown links as it may sometimes lead to cyberhacking.
  • Not mocking anyone on Social Media as it comes under cyber-bullying.
  • Never intrude on another person’s privacy.
  • Be polite and don’t use harsh words during online chats or communication.
  • Always install an anti-virus on your system.
  • Never install from unauthorised websites.
  • Do not copywrite any individual’s work and claim it to be yours.
  • Never compromise with the safety of your device.
